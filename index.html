Logan's Top Ten Mythical Animals

Number Name Image Description
1 Griffin A Griffin is a composite mythological creature with a lion's body and a bird's head, usually that of an eagle. In legend, griffins were guardians over great treasure and riches.
2 Oni In Japanese myth, oni are people who were so wicked in their lives that they transformed into demons in hell or while still alive. They have red or blue skin, horns, and tusks. They enact violent punishment on evil-doers and will eat just about anything, including humans.
3 Aqrabuamelu The aqrabuamelu, is a hybrid of a scorpion and a man, with the body of a scorpion and the face, torso, and arms of a man. While they are terrifying in appearance and were created to wage war against gods, they also stand as guardians and often warn travelers who might be in danger.
4 Minotaur The Minotaur had the body of a man with the head and tail of a bull. It feasts on human flesh and lurks within a maze – a maze which is as much a trap for its victims as it is for the beast itself.
5 Phoenix The phoenix is a mythical eagle-like creature, depicted as a large bird with gold and red feathers that could live for hundreds of years. The phoenix was a symbol of immortality, eternity, and resurrection.
6 Basilisk In European legend, the basilisk is a serpentine creature who, can kill with one look. It was hypothesized that basilisks could be killed by subjecting them to the odor of weasels, griffins’ tears, or seeing its own reflection.
7 Chimera In Greek mythology, the Chimera was a fire-breathing mythical creature, usually depicted as a lion merged with a goat and a snake. However, the term chimera is now often used to describe any creature that is a hybrid of multiple animals, often composed of wildly different parts.
8 Banshee Iin Irish folk tales, the banshee is a female spirit said to appear preceding the death of a loved one. She will wail or shriek to warn family members of the coming disaster.
9 Dybbuk The dybbuk is a Jewish folk tale of the ghost or dislocated soul of a dead person which then possesses a living human. This malevolent spirit is said to only depart from its host once it has achieved some sort of goal, allowing it to pass on.
10 Skin-Walker The Skinwalker is a kind of wicked sorcerer who can transform into, occupy, or disguise themselves as an animal. In other traditions, a person – man, woman, or child, becomes a skinwalker when they perform any kind of deep-seated taboo; similar to the wendigo curse.
